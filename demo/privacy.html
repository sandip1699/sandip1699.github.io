Privacy

At Inspired eSolutions, we are concerned about your privacy. Most of our Web pages can be visited without leaving any personal information about yourself. We will not disclose information regarding you (such as name, address and phone number) to any outside organization for its use in marketing or solicitation without your consent.

You are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords or any account information. Please be careful and responsible whenever you're online.
